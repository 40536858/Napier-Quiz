Founding

Edinburgh Napier University was founded as Napier Technical College in 1964 by John Napier, a 16th century mathematician and philosopher.

ENSA

ENSA is the Edinburgh Napier Students' Association. ENSA aims to provide Napier students with several services including social facilities, quality advice, and support.

Campuses

Edinburgh Napier University has 3 campuses. These are Merchiston, Craiglockhart, and Sighthill. The Merchiston campus is built around the refurbished Merchiston Castle.

Awards

Edinburgh Napier is 601th out of 800 in the Times World University rankings. Napier has also been rated top modern university in both the Times and The Sunday Times Good University Guide 2021.
